Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often do you clean and service the portable toilets?

For all rentals, service is scheduled at least once a week. For high-traffic events or long-term construction projects, we can create a customized service schedule to meet your specific needs, including multiple services per week to ensure units remain clean and fully stocked.

2. Can I rent for just one day?

Yes. We offer one day porta potty rental for inspections, pop-up events, or emergencies—with no long-term commitment.

3. Do you deliver to rural areas?

Absolutely. We serve remote farms, forests, and job sites nationwide, including hard-to-reach locations in Georgia and around Texas.

4. What happens if my event is canceled?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before delivery. After that, a small restocking fee applies for custom or luxury units.
